About me

My name is Aaron Key and I'm from South Carolina. I've been interested in programming for a while but unsure of which language I wanted to learn, as well as how to get into it. I had read a few books on C and Python and wrote a few simple things with them but it never really caught my interest.

In March of 2016 I stumbled across FreeCodeCamp and began the lessons there for HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I loved it! At first the projects seemed challenging because they don't hold your hand the whole way; I had to do a lot of research outside of their website to learn how to do things. Doing so vastly increased my drive to learn, and expanded my knowledge.

Asking others for help, and spending hours reading through Mozilla Developer Network, and tons of other libraries and API documentations, I've learned quite a bit on how Front End Development works.

I currently work as a technician for a battery and power-tool producer, but hope to start doing freelance work soon, and move up to a full-time web-development career!
